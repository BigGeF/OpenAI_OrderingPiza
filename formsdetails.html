MinT Practice Interview Interest Form
Thank you for your interest in practice technical interviews! Once you complete this form, you will be contacted by FloCareer to confirm your practice interview time and learn what to expect. Make sure flocareer.com is saved to your safe sender list, and check all junk folders in case emails are mistakenly delivered there.

In your interview, a professional interviewer will provide a highly realistic virtual interview experience. Treat this like a real first round technical interview. At the end of your session, you will receive verbal feedback from your interviewer, and their written feedback will also be emailed to you and your program (MinT staff and your faculty/program staff as appropriate) so you can use it to prioritize your job search and interview preparation.
